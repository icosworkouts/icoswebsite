About Icos Workouts

Icos Workouts is a Gym finding and Gym management application for gym owners and clients.

A control panel for customer side and owner side application for customers to scan when entering the gym, track people in the gym in real-time, get membership updates and offers, search for relevant gym, view gym interior images, view each gym equpment, check gym timing and other important information

Owners get power to see people currently in the gym in real-time, get history of any customer, send notifications, modify gym timing, modify gym packs, add other staff members with limited accessibility
